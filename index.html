Jingjing Deng     邓晶晶

  Lecturer (Assistant Professor), Ph.D

  Vision & Machine Learning Lab, Rand2AI

  Computer Science, Swansea University, UK

  

I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Swansea University, UK. My research interests are in theoretical machine learning and artificial intelligence in general. In recent years, I focus on studying generative models and their applications in computer vision, medical image analysis and bioinformatics. I am open to collaborate across those disciplines with partners from both academic and industry.
