Jingjing Deng     邓晶晶

  Lecturer (Assistant Professor), Ph.D, FHEA

  Vision & Machine Learning Lab, Rand2AI

  Computer Science, Swansea University, UK

  

I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Swansea University, UK. My research interests are in theoretical machine learning and artificial intelligence in general. I focus on studying probabilistic generative models, theories for deep neural networks and applications in medical image analysis and bioinformatics. I am open to and actively seeking academic and industrial collaboration on interdisciplinary challenges.
