Jingjing Deng     邓晶晶

  Lecturer (Assistant Professor), Ph.D

  Vision & Machine Learning Lab, Rand2AI

  Computer Science, Swansea University, UK

  

I joined Department of Computer Science in 2018 as a Lecturer. Formerly I obtained my PhD degree in 2017, then did postdoctoral work in Vision and Machine Learning Lab and Visual Computing Group at Swansea University. My research interests are mainly in theoretical machine learning and its applications to computer vision and data mining problems.
